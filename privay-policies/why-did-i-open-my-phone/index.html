Privacy Policy

Why Did I Open My Phone?

Last Updated: March 8, 2026

Thank you for using Why Did I Open My Phone?. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information.

1. Information We Collect

The app is designed to respect your privacy.

Personal Information

  • The app does not collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location.

Usage Data

  • The app may store the following information locally on your device:
    • Your logged intentions (why you opened your phone)
    • Timestamps of when the app was opened
    • Focus scores and streak data
    • App usage statistics

This information is used only to provide features within the app.

2. How We Use Information

All stored information is used solely to provide the core functionality of the app, including:

  • Tracking your intention logs
  • Calculating focus scores
  • Displaying usage insights and reports
  • Maintaining streaks and progress tracking

Your data is not transmitted to external servers.

3. Data Storage

All app data is stored locally on your device.

We do not store user data on our servers or cloud services.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your data with third parties.

Your information remains private and accessible only on your device.

5. Third-Party Services

The app may use standard Android system services required for normal functionality.

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

6. Data Security

Because all data is stored locally on your device, you maintain full control over your information.

However, please be aware that no method of electronic storage is completely secure.

7. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Choices

You may:

  • Delete your data by clearing the app's storage or uninstalling the app
  • Export your data if the app provides an export feature

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected by changing the "Last Updated" date.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact:

Email: support@yourappdomain.com

By using Why Did I Open My Phone?, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
