TraceLearn Kids ("the App") is developed, created, and hosted by **NUC7 Studios**. We are committed to protecting the privacy of children and all users of our educational apps.

## 1. 100% Offline Nature

The App is designed to work completely offline. It does not require an internet connection to function and does not communicate over the internet at any time.

## 2. No Data Collection

**NUC7 Studios does not collect, store, or share any personal information.**

- We do not ask for your name, email, or any other personal identifiers.
- We do not use any tracking technologies (like cookies or device IDs).
- Your progress in the app (for example, letters traced) is stored strictly on your local device and is never sent to our servers.

## 3. No Advertisements or In-App Purchases

TraceLearn Kids is a completely free application.

- No ads are shown within the app.
- No in-app purchases are available.

## 4. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

As an educational app for kids, we adhere strictly to the Child Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA). Since we do not collect any data at all, we do not collect any personal information from children under the age of 13.

## 5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at **support@nuc7.com**.

By using **TraceLearn Kids**, you agree to this Privacy Policy.
