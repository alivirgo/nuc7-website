A child who is learning to trace, count, or recognize letters needs a calm path through the activity. Interruptions can make the practice feel less like learning and more like random screen time.

Parents should review each app's current policy and store listing before making it part of a daily routine. The NUC7 site keeps this kind of information close to the app pages.
