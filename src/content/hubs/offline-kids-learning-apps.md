Offline-friendly learning is less about avoiding technology and more about keeping the experience controlled. A child can practice letters, tracing, or numbers without needing a stream of unrelated online content.

Parents should still check each app's current store listing and privacy policy, but the guiding principle is simple: the core practice should be calm, predictable, and easy to supervise.
