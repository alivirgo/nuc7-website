Home-schooling does not require every tool to be large. Sometimes a parent needs a focused app that gives the child one small task and makes it easy to repeat.

The NUC7 kids lineup is shaped around those small tasks. A home routine can start with one app, one skill, and one follow-up activity away from the screen.
