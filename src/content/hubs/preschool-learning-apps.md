Preschool apps should make a child feel capable, not rushed. The most useful app is often the one that helps a child repeat a small skill with a little more confidence each day.

A good preschool routine might be five minutes of tracing, a spoken word game, or counting nearby objects. NUC7 pages are organized so parents can find the app that matches that simple daily target.
