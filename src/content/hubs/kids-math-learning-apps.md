Early math should feel concrete. Young children often understand numbers better when a parent connects app practice to objects they can touch: blocks, snacks, toys, stairs, crayons, or fingers.

Math 1 is the NUC7 early numeracy app direction. Its role is to support first math confidence through short home routines, not to rush a child into advanced problem solving.
