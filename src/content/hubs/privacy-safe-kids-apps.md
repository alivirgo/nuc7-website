Privacy is part of the product experience for parents. A learning app can look cheerful, but parents still need to know whether the app is public, what its policy says, and where to find support.

NUC7 keeps privacy-policy routes visible because families should not have to hunt for trust signals. This page helps parents evaluate safety questions before choosing an educational app.
