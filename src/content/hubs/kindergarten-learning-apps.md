Kindergarten readiness is not about cramming. It is about comfort with the early building blocks a child will meet again and again: lines, shapes, letters, sounds, counting, and simple instructions.

NUC7 apps are organized by these building blocks so parents can choose a focused practice path and avoid overwhelming the child with too many new demands at once.
