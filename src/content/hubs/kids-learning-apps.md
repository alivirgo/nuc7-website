The NUC7 kids line is built around a simple idea: a young learner benefits from clear, repeatable practice more than from a crowded app that tries to do every subject at once.

Parents can use this page as a starting map. If the child needs hand-control and tracing confidence, start with TraceLearn Kids. If the child needs early English vocabulary, start with English 1. If the child is ready for number recognition and counting, follow Math 1 as it moves through closed testing. If the parent wants conversation and discovery prompts, watch Curiosity 1.
