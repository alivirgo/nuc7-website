English 1 is part of the MnM Home Schooling line, aimed at giving families a simple, low-friction way to practice language basics at home.

The app is shaped for very early learners, so the design priority is calm repetition rather than feature sprawl. It is meant to feel approachable for children and easy to understand for parents who just want to start a quick practice session.
