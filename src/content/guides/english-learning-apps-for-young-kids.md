Early English learning should feel connected to the child's world. A word becomes more memorable when the child hears it, sees it, says it, and connects it to something nearby.

English 1 gives parents a simple NUC7 path for early language practice. Use it briefly, then ask the child to repeat a word, find an object, or trace a related shape.
