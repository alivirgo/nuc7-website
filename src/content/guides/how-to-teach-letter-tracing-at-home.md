Letter tracing starts before letters look perfect. Children first need to understand movement: down, across, around, curve, stop, and try again.

A practical home routine can be very short. Open TraceLearn Kids for guided practice, choose one motion or letter, then repeat that same motion on paper. Parents can say the motion out loud while the child follows it. That turns a screen task into a shared learning moment.
