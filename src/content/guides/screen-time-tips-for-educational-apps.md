The problem with screen time is often not the screen itself, but the lack of a clear endpoint. A learning app becomes more useful when the parent knows why it is being opened and when the session should end.

Use one small goal: trace one shape, say three words, count five objects, or answer one curiosity prompt. Then close the app and keep the learning going in the room.
