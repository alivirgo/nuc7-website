By age 4, many children are ready for a little more structure. They may begin connecting tracing motions to letters, numbers, and early writing confidence.

TraceLearn Kids is the clearest tracing-first NUC7 option. Parents can use it for a short session, then ask the child to trace one favorite shape or letter on paper. That repetition helps the app practice become a real skill, not just a screen action.
