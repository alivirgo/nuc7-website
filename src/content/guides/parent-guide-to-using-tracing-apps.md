Tracing apps are most useful when a parent treats them as a guided starting point. The app can show the motion and give the child a repeatable task, but handwriting confidence still grows through encouragement and real-world repetition.

Use the same motion in two places: once on the screen, once away from the screen. That tiny bridge makes tracing practice feel less abstract.
