Long learning sessions are not always better for young children. A short routine can become powerful when it is easy to repeat.

Parents can use a simple rhythm: choose one skill, open the matching app, complete a small activity, and ask one follow-up question. This keeps app time intentional and prevents practice from turning into endless screen time.
