At age 3, tracing is usually about comfort with motion, not perfect letter formation. Parents should look for activities that let the child follow simple paths and feel successful.

TraceLearn Kids is a sensible NUC7 starting point because it keeps the focus on tracing and recognition. A parent can extend the session by drawing one big line on paper and asking the child to follow it with a finger, crayon, or pencil.
