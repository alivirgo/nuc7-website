Kindergarten readiness is not one single skill. A child benefits from early comfort with tracing, recognizing letters and numbers, following simple prompts, and finishing small tasks.

NUC7 pages are organized around those skills. Parents can choose a tracing-first, language-first, or number-first path and use the app as a short daily practice tool.
