Curiosity grows when children are allowed to notice, wonder, and talk. Parents can support that with small prompts during ordinary moments: what do you see, what changed, what do you think will happen?

Curiosity 1 is the NUC7 direction for discovery-focused learning. It is different from tracing or math practice because the goal is broader observation and parent-guided exploration.
