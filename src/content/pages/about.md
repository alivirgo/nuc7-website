title: A small studio with two clear lanes.
summary: NUC7 Studios is being rebuilt as a focused app studio for kids home-schooling and parent productivity.
---

NUC7 Studios is organized around two audiences that need very different emotional textures from software.

## Kids Home Schooling

The kids line is built for home practice, parent trust, and repeat use. The priority is not just "educational content," but the feeling of a child returning to the app without friction and a parent understanding what the tool is for right away.

## Parent Productivity

The parent productivity line is for attention, follow-through, and clearer device use in days that already ask a lot from a family. These products should feel practical, direct, and calm rather than loud or motivational for its own sake.

## Why this split matters

Both lines care about focus, but they solve different problems. One supports early learning habits. The other protects parent attention in modern digital life. Keeping that distinction visible helps the website, the catalog, and future content stay coherent.
