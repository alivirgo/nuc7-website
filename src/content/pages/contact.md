For product support, release questions, or privacy-policy clarifications, email **support@nuc7.com**.

## Helpful details to include

- The exact app name
- The device model and Android version, if relevant
- A short description of what happened
- Screenshots if the issue is visual or hard to describe

## Best use of this inbox

This address works best for support, policy questions, and release communication. It is also the right place to send clarification requests when a closed-testing app needs legal or store-listing follow-up before launch.
